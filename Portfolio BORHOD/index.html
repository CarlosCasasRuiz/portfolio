Why BROHOD?

This is the name of a personal project that was born two years ago due to the pandemic situation that we are living. this was created for and by the people who make everything work, which are the local commerce. I grew up in a rural and little-industralized area and the COVID situation caused many companies to be misplaced.

it was time to act and help these people, who had created a business out of nothing, with everything they had.

So, I adopted that position of elder brother who is always there to help you make it out of any problem. This initiative grew fast and this is how I become BROHOD.
